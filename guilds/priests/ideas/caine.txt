    
Caine, the Demigod of Power
+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+*+
 
Caine was the first son of Boccob. Unline Boccob, he was openly aggressive, 
even inhuman, yet noble-minded. His idea was to convert the heathens with
force. Should they resist this crusade, they were to put to death. However,
Boccob disagreed, and told him why all life should be respected. Caine
understood, but he had his own ideas about the Holy Order...
